    • OUR STORY

    One of every five children,teens and adults in India are diagonised with mental illness. And they suffer from depression, anxiety issues where they fail to open up to their closed ones which affect their as well as their closed ones' lives.

    While doctor, therapist and medicine are necessary, but they are not enough. Suicide rates continue to rise especially among teens who can't handle much academic or parental or peer pressure.

    When someone's life is interrupted by mental illness it can be tough to see a practical way forward with day to day life and a good future seems out of reach.

    During such times people apart from medical help need emotional help which generally can't be provided by their closed ones as they themselves haven't gone through similar conditions. Here comes our website "Peace Seekers" where people who are suffering from similar kind of issues can interact with each other and can rely on someone they don't know as it's said that sometimes talking to a complete stranger feels better than talking to someone you know as they would never judge you and can be vulnerable to each other.

    We want to provide a platform where people can themselves get an idea of what is actually happening to their mind, can interact with similar people and can get hope andand try to uplift themselves without any treatment. People can get specialised doctor's help and can attend meditation sessions through this platform. We intend to support people who have completely lost their hopes and spread their life with happiness and peace .

    OUR MISSION

    To empower individuals to live a full and rich faith-filled life and giving them assurance that they are not alone .

    OUR VISION

    At "Peace Seekers" our vision is to create a compassionate and supportive community where individuals battling depression can find solace and strength. We believe in the power of connection and the importance of understanding mental health. Our goal is to provide a safe space for individuals to share their experiences, find support from peers, and gain insights into their mental well-being.

    Through our support groups, we aim to foster meaningful conversations and build a network of care, ensuring that no one has to face their struggles alone. Our questionnaire section is designed to empower individuals with knowledge about their mental health status, offering guidance and resources tailored to their needs.

    We envision a world where mental health is prioritized, and everyone has access to the support and tools they need to lead a fulfilling and balanced life. Join us in our mission to break the stigma surrounding depression and create a brighter, healthier future for all.

    OUR PHILOSOPHY

    1. Empathy and Compassion: We believe in the power of empathy and compassion to heal. Every individual deserves to be heard, understood, and supported without judgment.

    2. Community Support: Connecting with others who share similar experiences can provide immense relief and strength. Our support groups are designed to foster a sense of belonging and mutual encouragement.

    3. Holistic Well-being: Mental health is a vital part of overall well-being. We promote a holistic approach that includes emotional, mental, and physical health.

    4. Accessibility and Inclusivity: Everyone should have access to mental health resources, regardless of their background or circumstances. We strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment for all.

    5. Confidentiality and Safety: We prioritize the privacy and safety of our users. Our platform ensures that all interactions and shared information are kept confidential.

    6. Continuous Improvement: Mental health care is an evolving field. We are committed to continually improving our services and incorporating the latest research and best practices.
